About Me

I am a Web Developer and Software Tester with over two years of professional experience, mostly in Quality Assurance roles. My background combines software development projects, hands-on manual testing across multiple platforms, and building automated testing solutions in languages such as Python and GoLang.

During my first internship at uTalk, a language-learning resource provider, my main two projects were developing a Data Analytics application in Python and a canvas-based version of the uTalk app in Javascript. I also worked on manually testing the app across various devices.

My first internship mostly consisted of manual testing and maintaining Selenium tests. My second internship consisted of a database-testing project using MySQL queries integrated into Python tests, along with a small amount of manual testing. The year I spent working full-time at Thought Machine during the pandemic mostly involved writing, maintaining and running test suites in GoLang.

I studied Computer Science and AI at the University of Sussex. Unfortunately, owing to a number of factors including the Covid lockdown, mental health issues and my dissertation supervisor leaving the University, I dropped out of the third year of my degree. I am considering taking a Top-Up year at Aberystwyth University to complete my degree.
