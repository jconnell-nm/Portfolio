About Me

I’m a Web Developer and Software Tester with over two years of professional experience, specialising in Quality Assurance. I bring a hybrid skill set that combines development, hands-on cross-platform testing, and building automated test solutions using Python and Go.

At uTalk, a language-learning platform, I contributed to both development and QA. I built a Python-based data analytics tool, developed a canvas-driven version of the app in JavaScript, and carried out manual testing across a wide range of devices.

Across my internships and professional experience, I’ve worked extensively in both manual and automated testing. I’ve maintained and expanded Selenium test suites, developed database testing frameworks using MySQL integrated with Python, and written and executed robust test suites in Go during my time at Thought Machine.

I studied Computer Science and AI at the University of Sussex, and I’m currently considering completing my degree via a Top-Up year at Aberystwyth University.
